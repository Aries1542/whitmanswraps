How It Began

This all began on a typical back day at the gym. I was fired up for some lat pulldowns, using the standard lifting straps everyone knows. But the process? It took me an entire two minutes just to get the straps on, only to realize I had set the weight completely wrong. Frustrated, I took the straps off, thinking, There has to be a better way.

I turned to Google, convinced someone had already solved this problem. To my surprise, I couldn't find anything that made lifting straps both easy to use, and effective. That's when I decided to take matters into my own hands, literally. I went home and created the very first version of what would become Whitmans Wraps.
